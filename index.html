Phoenix

Chinese-born U.S.-based rapper Bohan Phoenix is making some noise in the global hip-hop scene with his bilingual rhymes and fine blend of East and West elements in his productions and lyrics.

Having spent half of his life in China and half in the States, Bohan's music is unique in its way of blending the two languages, culture, and music elements into one.
